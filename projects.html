Our projects

Practical action for forests, communities, and future generations.

RootRise creates projects that restore ecosystems while giving communities the knowledge and tools to protect forests long term.

What we support

Projects that grow lasting change

01

Sustainable Nurseries

RootRise helps communities create nurseries where native seedlings can be grown, cared for, and prepared for reforestation projects.

02

Reforestation Programmes

Our reforestation programmes focus on planting native trees in areas affected by deforestation and supporting their long-term growth.

03

School Forest Education

RootRise works with schools to teach children why forests matter and how young people can become environmental advocates.

04

Ecosystem Restoration

We support restoration methods that improve soil health, biodiversity, water protection, and the natural balance of forest ecosystems.

How projects work

From seedling to forest

Plan

We work with communities to understand local needs, damaged areas, and the best native species to grow.

Grow

Sustainable nurseries raise healthy seedlings and create local skills for future restoration.

Restore

Trees are planted, cared for, and monitored so forests can continue to grow over time.
