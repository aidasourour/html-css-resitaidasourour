About RootRise

Rooted in hope, grown through community.

RootRise was founded by Jessica, whose childhood love for forests grew into a mission to protect nature and restore damaged ecosystems.

Our beginning

Jessica’s story

RootRise began with Jessica’s deep connection to forests. As a child, she found forests peaceful, inspiring, and full of life. As she grew older, her concern about deforestation became stronger.

She wanted to create more than a tree-planting project. She wanted to build a movement that helps communities reconnect with nature and protect their local environments for future generations.

Our mission

Restoring ecosystems with people at the centre

RootRise partners with local communities in deforested regions. Through training, sustainable nurseries, and education, communities are empowered to become guardians of their own forests.

Our values

What guides our work

Community Empowerment

We believe restoration is strongest when local people have the skills, tools, and confidence to care for their own forests.

Education

We work with schools to help children understand the vital role forests play in protecting ecosystems and supporting life.

Long-term Restoration

We focus on sustainable change by supporting nurseries, native planting, and ongoing forest care.
