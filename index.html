Our purpose

A movement for forests and people

RootRise is more than a tree-planting initiative. It is a movement that helps people reconnect with nature while restoring forests that have been damaged by deforestation.

By working with local communities and schools, RootRise supports long-term environmental change through education, training, and practical action.

What we do

Growing change from the ground up

Community Nurseries

We help local communities establish sustainable nurseries that provide young trees for restoration projects.

Forest Restoration

We restore damaged ecosystems by planting native trees and supporting long-term care for growing forests.

Environmental Education

We work with schools to teach children about the importance of forests and inspire them to protect nature.
